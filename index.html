Many issues of Advances in Nutrition include Nutrient Information articles, concise summaries of essential facts about nutrients including information on food sources, diet recommendations, deficiencies, toxicity, clinical uses, recent research and references for further information for micro- and macronutrients.

Design and Conduct of Human Nutrition Randomized Controlled Trials

Human nutrition randomized controlled trials (RCTs) are vital for establishing causal relations between exposure to nutrients, food, or dietary patterns and prespecified outcomes, such as body composition, biomarkers, or event rates.
